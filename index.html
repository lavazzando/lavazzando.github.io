Beyond acoustic resolution.

I work with violins, electronics and installation to explore unstable listening situations, glitches, and almost-imperceptible changes in sound. My practice moves between concert, exhibition and research, often in collaboration with other artists and scientists.

I am currently based in Köln, working on performances and sound installations around quantum-inspired soundscapes and extended string techniques.
